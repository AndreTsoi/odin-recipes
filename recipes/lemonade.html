Carbonara

Description

Lemonade is easy to make, but getting the ratios right is very important. This is a simple to follow along step-by-step lemonade recipe that will taste delicious.

Ingredients

  • Lemons
  • Sugar
  • Water

Steps

  1. Combine 1 cup each of water and sugar
  2. Cook mixture over medium heat until sugar dissolves
  3. Cool syrup for 10 minutes
  4. Combine syrup and lemon juice in a pitcher
  5. Add remaining water and pour over ice
